Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026
Short version: We don't store your prompts on any server. Everything stays on your device. We only use Microsoft Clarity for anonymous usage analytics.

1. What We Collect

DataWhere StoredWhy
Prompt libraryYour device (localStorage)So you can save and reuse prompts
Language preferenceYour device (localStorage)Remember your language setting
Usage stats (count, scores)Your device (localStorage)Show your personal stats on home page
Anonymous usage dataMicrosoft ClarityUnderstand how the app is used (no personal info)

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store your prompts on any server
  • We do not collect your name, email or phone number
  • We do not create user accounts or profiles
  • We do not sell any data to third parties
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not collect payment information (app is free)

3. How Your Prompts Are Processed

When you type a prompt and Clarix enhances it, your text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. This is necessary for the core functionality of Clarix. Anthropic processes your text according to their own privacy policy and terms of service. We recommend reviewing Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

4. Microsoft Clarity Analytics

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how users interact with Clarix. This service collects anonymous heatmaps and session recordings. It does not collect personally identifiable information. You can opt out of Clarity by using a browser with tracking protection enabled. Review Microsoft's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Local Storage

Clarix stores your preferences and saved prompts in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or using the browser's developer tools.

6. Children's Privacy

Clarix is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used Clarix in a way that concerns you, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete on our end. All your data is stored locally on your own device — you have complete control and can delete it at any time.

8. Security

All communication between Clarix and the Anthropic API is encrypted via HTTPS. We do not transmit your data over unencrypted connections. Your locally stored data is as secure as your own device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the new policy.
